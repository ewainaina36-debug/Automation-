MQTT Protocol

📡

What is MQTT?

MQTT (Message Queuing Telemetry Transport) is a lightweight, publish-subscribe messaging protocol designed for IoT devices and constrained networks.

  • Lightweight & efficient
  • Publish-Subscribe model
  • Minimal bandwidth usage
  • Quality of Service levels
  • Secure SSL/TLS support
🔄

How it Works

Devices publish messages to topics on a central broker. Other devices subscribe to topics of interest, creating a dynamic, decoupled communication network.

  • Client-Broker architecture
  • Topic-based routing
  • Message persistence options
  • Last Will & Testament
  • Automatic reconnection
